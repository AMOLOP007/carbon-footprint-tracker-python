About Aetherra

Aetherra is a comprehensive Corporate Carbon Intelligence platform engineered to seamlessly track, measure, and analyze scope emissions across multiple operational domains including Technology, Manufacturing, Logistics, and Construction.

By integrating real-time Grid Intensity analysis via API, deep AI-powered extraction from standard corporate PDF reports, and intelligent dynamic visualizations, Aetherra empowers organizations to effortlessly transform isolated operational data into actionable, audit-ready sustainability metrics.
